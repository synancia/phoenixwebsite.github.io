About us

We are a well-established language school with twenty years of experience in preparing students for univeristy entrance exams. We offer language courses in small classes as well as private tuition. We are native speakers, well experienced in language teaching. Respected examniers and Greek speaking, successful educators in our field.










COURSES



PRE-JUNIOR

These courses are aimed at children who are in their initial stages of English language learning. They enable pupils to use English effectively for their own personal, concrete world while having fun.



ENGLISH AND GERMAN ALL LEVELS A1-C2          

These courses aim to develop and enhance the integration of the skills of listening, speaking, reading and writing through lessons which involve task-based activities.



IELTS        

The IELTS intensive course offers thorough preparation for the exam covering the four skills -listening, reading, writing, speaking.Also, it deals with exam format questions and gives guidelines.



IGCSE        

The IGCSE (ESL) course is genuinely educational and aims to develop students both intellectually and linguistically, to increase personal awareness and to encourage international understanding.



CAMBRIDGE EXAMS UK        

Cambridge English exams are recognised by over 20,000 universities, employers and governments around the world.



ANGLIA EXAMS UK        

Anglia Exams offers a comprehensive and structured programme of assessing English language competence, from beginner through to full competence as an expert user.
